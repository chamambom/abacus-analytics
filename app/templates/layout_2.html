  • Our Story
    • This site is about one area of Data science called data analytics (Trending, Predictive, Comparative) in the ISP industry.Our job is to collect data from ISP customers and analyse that data with the purpose of drawing conclusions about that information.

      Over time we have seen a proliferation of ISPs in Zimbabwe yet there is no information regarding how the ISPs performance is perceived.We have created a platform for the Zimbabwean Internet user community to provide us with data that will enable us to create a community driven Reputation database which will enable us to benchmark ISP health Index based on defined KPIs and guide prospective subscribers/customers/users to make informed decisions before they commit to any ISP.

      In summary this aims to create a unit of purpose for Zimbabwean ISPs, establish and maintain best of breed practices, and ultimately try to improve their service offerings for the general public

      In its raw fomart , this data will also be available via splunk , a tool that we use to generate visual graphs and this portal will be accessible at a fee to ISPs ,Regulators and anyone interested in that data.

      Technologies used in this project - Flask ,Flask-SQLAlchemy ,PyGAL for graphing and React for the front end

      please review below What is Data Science and how is it different from statistics and analytics? it spans fields like computer science, modeling, statistics, analytics, and mathematics. At its core, data science involves using automated methods to analyze massive amounts of data and to extract knowledge from them. By combining aspects of statistics, computer science, applied mathematics, and visualization, data science can turn the vast amounts of data the digital age generates into new insights and new knowledge.
